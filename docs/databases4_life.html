Databases for Life
 

The underlying Concept is that as we progress from being babies to Senior Citizens we go through increasingly complex Passages in life.
This increasing complexity can be reflected in a series Data Models that can be used to learn.


  Here is a series of Databases for Life
 

Databases for Passages in Life
