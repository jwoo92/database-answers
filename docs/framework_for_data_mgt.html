Framework for Data Management   

Layered Data Architecture

     
Data Governance ensures compliance with corporate policies and
procedures that effect Enterprise Data Management.


This is the 'BI Layer' that produces Reports, Mobile KPIs and 'Self-Service BI'.


Data Marts are Dimensional Models for specific business areas such as customers or Sales.

This layer is an Integration Platform that includes a wide range of functionality
- ETL, MDM, a 'Single View of the Truth'


This is the Staging Areas where data extract files are loaded 'As-Is' directly into temporary tables.

The Information Catalogue is very important and is often not given the priority that it deserves.
It stores a wide range of Information, including key Roles, such as
Data Stewards, Validation criteria and Transformation procedures for Source Data.
