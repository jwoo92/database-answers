  Our Commitment to the UN Global Compact

We signed up for the United Nations Global Compact.

Our commitment was to develop an ICT Toolkit to support Best Practice, Communities of Users and to help in sharing plans and experiences. For example, we have prepared a list of Case Studies and a summary of what two organisations are involved in.


  Our Accomplishments

Best Practice is supported, with Feedback to encourage collaboration.

e-Learning, Standard Operating Procedures and Tutorials can also be supported using the same Approach.

Users can join Communities of common interests.

An Information Catalog provides one single place where details of all available Information is provided.

  Our Current Work

We are currently adding Performance Metrics to integrate measurement of the Quality of Life as part of Development Programmes.
