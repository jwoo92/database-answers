The Requirements were drafted from an article in the Financial Times Weekend Magazine for December 19/20, 2009.
The article featured the Chiddingfold Hunt in West Sussex, which is where I used to live.
I also looked at the Wikipedia entry for Fox Hunting.

Fox Hunting is an example of the 'Usual Suspects' Data Model.

This is an example of the Usual Suspects Generic Data Model and we have created some specific examples of it, including :-
  • Conflicts and Wars
  • Football Clubs
  • Fox Hunting
  • Wedding Parties

    A Data Mart has been created to help with Enquires and Reports.
