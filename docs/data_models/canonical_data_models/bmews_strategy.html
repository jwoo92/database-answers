The Database Answers BMEWS Development Strategy

BMEWS stands for 'Business Monitoring and Early Warning System' and is based on Key Performance Indicators ('KPIs')

Our aproach is based on designing Generic Solutions to a range of Applications with similar Requirements.

We can see, for example, that both Recruitment and Talent Agencies have Skills in addition to the basic Customer and Services Entities.
All Applications will adopt a common approach to Business Intelligence ('BI') with Reports that are based on the Generic design, using
Translation Tables to translate the generic terms used.

The Reports will take data from a Data Mart, based on this generic design for a Customers and Products Data Mart.
