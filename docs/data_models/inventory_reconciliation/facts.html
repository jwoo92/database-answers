requests, here are the business requirements for my database that I need:

�A small manufacturer creates a number of products (multiple SKUs).

�Each of these products are sold in a variety of retail outlets.
�Each retail outlet has a display for our products.
�Each product goes into a display.
�Some retailers also offer "storage space" in their warehouse, for inventory that cannot fit on the display.
�A merchandiser (employed by manufacturer) goes to retailers and does an inventory count (of everything in that store).

�If the display stand has been depleted and there is inventory in storage, he replenishes the display stand from the storage inventory.

�Inventory in both display & storage levels need to be monitored separately.
�If the inventory has hit pre-defined reorder levels, he submits a re-order.
�The company processes the order and dispatches it for delivery.
�Upon delivery, the display is restocked (by the merchandiser) and excess inventory put in storage

The dates for each time an inventory check is done (i.e. a "reconciliation") should also be stored.
