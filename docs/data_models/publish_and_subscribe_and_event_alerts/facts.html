Data Model for Publish and Subscribe and Event Alerts 
Back to the Data Model. 
The Draft Business Rules haved been defined for discussion purposes :-

  • Subscribers enter into Subscriptions

  • Subscription Actions are Event Alerts and are generated when the appropriate conditions are met.

  • A Monitor Log is maintained of all Subscription Actions.
    • A draft description of the Events and Alerts :-
    Conventional Events are monitored and when Threshold values are reached, an Alert is triggered.
    When this happens, people who have registered for the appropriate Subscription are notified.
