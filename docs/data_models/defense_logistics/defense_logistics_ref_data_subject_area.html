3. A Data Model for Defense Logistics Reference Data

  • In Britain, the UK National Codification Bureau defines the NATO standards used within the Ministry of Defence.
  • Here's the MOD answer to the question What is Source Data ? -
    "Source Data is the technical information required to codify fully an item of supply.
    It can be a drawing, specification or standard, or propriety information from a manufacturer's catalogue."
