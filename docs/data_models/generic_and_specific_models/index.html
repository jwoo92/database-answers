These Generic and Specific Data Models are very important because they illustrate how a number of specific Data Models can be incorporated into one Generic Model.
In this case, the concept of a Life-Cycle from the Cradle to the Grave is used to generate these Scenarios :-
1. Baby (Me and Mommy)
2. Teenager (Traffic Cops and Tickets
3. Student (Behaviour Monitoring)
4. Adult (Partnerships and Relationships)
5. Adult (Golf Club Tournament)
6. Senior Citizen (Health Centres)
