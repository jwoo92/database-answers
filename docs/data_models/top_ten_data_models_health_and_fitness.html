Welcome to the Health and Fitness Data Models Page
  

   This Page will help you if you want a series of Data Models in an Health and Fitness Theme.
  This Page will help you get Started
 

A set of Health and Fitness Data Models.
 
1 Athletes and Events Includes Athletes, Clubs, Events and Gender.
2 Athletes and Teams Includes Divisions, Letters, Sports, Student Athletes, Students in Events and Students in Teams.
3 Ballet Companies Includes Addresses, Contacts, Donations, Gifts, Organisations, Parties, Pledges and Staff.
4 Clinical Trials Includes Drugs, Individuals, Investigators and Trials.
5 Dance School Includes Addresses, Classes, Exams, Fees, Students and Teachers.
6 Health Centers Includes Appointments, Medication, Patients and Staff.
7 Health Insurance Claims Includes Claims, Hospitals, Insurance Companies, Patients and Staff.
8 Nursing Home Includes Billable Items, Care Units, Insurance Companies, Patients, Rooms, Staff and Tests.
9 Olympic Sports Includes Accommodation, Competitors, Events, Results and Venues.
10 Sports Centers Includes Bookings, Facilities, Sports Centers and Users.
10 Data Warehouse Based on the Olympic Sports Data Model.
