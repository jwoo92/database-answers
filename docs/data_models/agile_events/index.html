Commentary
This style of Model is unique to Database Answers.
The intention is to provide an alternative approach to top-level modelling.

The 8 yellow boxes on the left are 'clickable' and provide a different way
to access information about Events.

These 8 can be changed for different sets of 8 to meet different requirements,
such as Financial Services or HR.

If you would like details please contact me or email at "info (at) barryw.org".

understand the Data Model.

for preparing KPIs and Performance Reports.
