The Area being Modelled is :-
Equipment Repair.

The Business Rules have been drafted by Barry as follows :-
1. Clients can place Work Orders.
2. Details are stored in the Work_Orders Table.
3. Repair Work is usually predefined and new ones can be generated as required.
4. Each Repair Work activity is broken down into a series of Repair Tasks.
5. Repair Work is broken down into a series of Tasks.
6. Each Task is related to the next Task to be done.
7. Each Repair Task uses a predefined amount of each piece of Equipment.
8. Equipment Repair Levels are recorded at regular intervals, usually every day.
