Data Models for the American Pickers TV Show 
An Access Database is available on demand. This Model is based on our Canonical Data Model.
There might also be a design for a Data Mart which is an excellent start for Enquiries and producing Reports.
If you are new to Data Models, this page of my new Tutorial will help you understand the Data Model.
The American Pickers TV Show features two guys (currently Mike and Frank) who drive around the back roads of
America looking for old and ancient Artefacts that they can buy and take back to their place in Iowa and sell and
make a profit so that they can go out and buy more Artefacts.

Both Mike and Frank have their special interests, such as old jukeboxes and pinball machines.
Mike has been known to buy things and then give them away because he is sympathetic to a cause, such as the
NASCAR Sports car Hall of Fame.
This helps to give the TV program a special warmth and a human touch.

For the first time I have added the Specifications for a Pickers Cloud Services and Data Platform.
These are stepping-stones to building a Cloud-based Service for Pickers. Email us if you would like some details.
This Service will help to keep track of things Mike and Frank buy and sell, and the money coming in and the money going out.
