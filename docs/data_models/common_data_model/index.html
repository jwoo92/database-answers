If you are new to Data Models, this page of my new Tutorial will help you understand the Data Model.

To see how this Model might work in practice, try to think of some common Events in your daily life - such as "Go to the Supermarket".
In this case, some of the Entities are :-
  • You are the Customer
  • The Document is the Sales Receipt.
  • The Location is where the store is located.
  • The Organisation is the Store or the Store owner.
  • The Products are what you put in your Shopping Basket
  • The Services are what the staff in the store do for you,

    You can try to work out what the Party is after you are comfortable with everything else ;-0)
