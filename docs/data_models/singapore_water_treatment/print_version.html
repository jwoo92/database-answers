Business Rules

We have drafted these Business Rules and created the Data Model accordingly.
Then we discuss these Rules with business users and modify the Data Model accordingly.
1. Customers (who can be Departments) ask for Jobs to be done.
2. Equipment is involved in each Job.
3. Staff, such as Engineers, are also involved in each Job.
4. Documentation, such as Receipts and Work Orders, is used with each Job.
5. Payments are made for each Job. These can be transfers of money between Departments.
