If you can see PF in the left-hand column of an Entity, such as Donations_to_Funds, it means that a Primary Key is also a Foreign Key to another Entity.

This usually appears as the result of the resolution of a Many-to-Many Relationship into two One-to-Many Relationships.
In this case, we can say that 'One Donation can be split among Many Churches' and 'One Church can receive Many Donations'.
