A Design Note on Primary Keys 设计说明的主键
My approach is very simple and always uses a system-generated unique number as a Primary Key, (except for Reference Data). 我的方法非常简单，始终使用系统生成的独一无二的号码作为主键， （除参考数据） 。
This is called a surrogate key, but there is a 'gotcha' which can be deadly. It is all too easy to enter the same record twice. Therefore you must always be sure to enforce a unique constraint on the natural key. Luckily this is easy to do in Access which can be a good basis for clarifying your design and constraints. 这就是所谓的替代的关键，但有一个' gotcha ' ，它是致命的。这是很容易进入同一记录的两倍。因此，一定要确保执行一个独特的自然制约因素的关键。幸运的，这是很容易做到在Access它可以是一个良好的基础，说明自己的设计和制约因素。

Why do I do this for free ? 为什么我这样做是免费的吗？
I design these Data Models for free to give something back to the Database community that has provided me with a good (and interesting) living for the past 15 years. 本人设计这些数据模型的自由回馈社会的数据库为我提供了一个良好的（有趣的）生活在过去的15年。

Each Model is usually what I can do in the 20 minutes each day of voluntary work that I allocate from my consulting work. 每一个模型通常是我唯一能做的事在20分钟，每一天的志愿工作，我分配给我的咨询工作。

My intention is to provide a wide range of 'Kick Start' Models that anyone can use as a starting-point, and could extend cleanly and logically, with appropriate reference to the Business Rules. 我的用意是提供广泛的'启动'模式，任何人都可以使用为出发点，并可以延长干净和逻辑，并提供适当的参考业务规则。

It is not my intention to provide Models that can be used off-the-shelf to meet the requirements of a large commercial organisation. 我不打算提供模型，可用于现成满足一个大的商业组织。
After all, that is one of the things I do for a living !!! 毕竟，这是一个东西我的生活！

None of the Models is the complete and final solution in its area, but any of them can be added to easily and quickly to meet a specific requirement. 无模式是完全和最终解决在其职权范围内，但任何人都可以添加到方便和快速地，以满足特定的要求。 The logic in each Model is correct and contains the minimum Entities for the area being modelled. It is easy to create a complex Model by combining these Kick-Start Models because they all follow the same design approach and standards. 逻辑在每个模型是正确的，并包含最低实体领域正在仿照。很容易创建一个复杂的模型相结合，这些启动模式，因为它们都遵循相同的设计方法和标准。

Generally, I try to make the Data Models and the associated Business Rules consistent, but I don't always define the Rules because that is a very valuable 'exercise for the student'. 一般来说，我试图使数据模型及相关业务规则一致，但我并不总是制定规则，因为这是一个非常宝贵的'运动的学生。
I'd be very interested in hearing any suggestions for improving this facility . Here's a first draft of a page of French Data Models . 我会非常感兴趣地听取任何 建议，为改善这一设施 。这里有一个初稿页法文数据模型 。
