  Business Intelligence
 
Business Intelligence can be defined as "deriving Information from Data".
  • Current Best Practice includes tailored User Portals, Dashboards and Key Performance Indicators,('KPIs').
  • For a Business Intelligence System, an enterprise needs to manage the data from End-to-End.
  • This requires quality assurance from the Source to the Target.
  • It is becoming more important for a 'Data Steward' to assume ownership of data at each stage in its processing. I have just (April 10th.2006) bought a book on Performance Dashboards by Wayne Eckerson,of TDWI, that gets 5 stars on Amazon.
