  A Generic version From Concept to Specifications
 
This diagram shows the underlying Framework that will guide the Users through the process of listening to a Presentation on a Concept, such as an Enterprise Information Architecture, and then adopting the Concept by following a Tutorial-type series of structured Steps.

This will produce Generic Specifications and finally these will be translated into Specifications tailored for vendor-specific products, such as IBM, Microsoft or Oracle.
