Using the IBM Virtual Innovation Center (VIC) Technical support

1) Go to the VIC Website
2) In center of the page, click the link titled "Sign in to the IBM Virtual Innovation Center"
3) You will then have to enter your PartnerWorld user ID and password
4) Upon successful logon, you should be at the IBM Virtual innovation Center home page
5) In the upper right hand corner, there is a box titled "We're here to help", this is our technical support
6) Online tech chat: chat real time with our technical mentors. Choose the language of choice and the IBM software product.
7) Email support - with this option, you can send an email to our technical group
